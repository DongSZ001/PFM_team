## Jing_2026_Uncovering_piezoelectric_effect_in_polycrystalline_diamond_membranes (2026)

**Title:** Uncovering piezoelectric effect in polycrystalline diamond membranes

**Authors:** Jixiang Jing, Bicong Wang, Yumeng Luo, Yicheng Wang, Zhongqiang Wang, Yiyao Liu, Dong-Keun Ki, Xinghua Shi, Qi Wang, Kwai Hei Li, Yuan Lin, Zhiqin Chu

**Journal:** Science Advances 12, eaea8318 (2026)

**Date:** 18 March 2026

**Abstract:** Diamonds have been regarded as nonpiezoelectric materials for more than one century. Here, we uncover a notable piezoelectric effect in ultrathin and ultraflexible polycrystalline diamond membranes. Our experiments show that the piezoelectricity depends on membrane thickness, with peak response (exhibiting a piezoelectric voltage coefficient of ~82.2 millivolt meters per newton that surpasses many conventional piezoelectric materials) seen in ~5-micrometer-thick membranes. First-principles calculations reveal that the unexpected piezoelectricity in polycrystalline membranes originates from local asymmetry induced by grain boundaries, which alter the electric polarization within a finite region near the boundary during deformation.

**Keywords:** piezoelectricity, polycrystalline diamond, grain boundaries, first-principles calculations, energy harvesting

## Zhang_2024_Large_Language_Model_Based_AI_Agent_for_Organic_Semiconductor_Device_Research (2024)

**Title:** Large-Language-Model-Based AI Agent for Organic Semiconductor Device Research

**Authors:** Qian Zhang, Yongxu Hu, Jiaxin Yan, Hengyue Zhang, Xinyi Xie, Jie Zhu, Huchao Li, Xinxin Niu, Liqiang Li, Yajing Sun, Wenping Hu

**Journal:** Advanced Materials 36, 2405163 (2024)

**Abstract:** An artificial intelligence (AI) agent for organic field-effect transistors (OFETs) is designed by integrating GPT-4 with well-trained ML algorithms. It can efficiently extract experimental parameters from scientific literature with precision and recall rates both exceeding 92%. Combined with ML models, this AI agent provides targeted guidance for device design. Using a database of 709 OFETs from 277 papers, a ML model based on XGBoost is trained for device performance judgment. The agent provided optimization scheme that tripled the charge transport properties of DP-DTT OFETs.

**Keywords:** LLM, AI agent, OFET, GPT-4, machine learning, text mining, organic semiconductor

**Abstract:** This Review presents key computational advances in materials design over the past decades. ## Tenreiro_2026_Flexoelectrically_Induced_Polar_Topology_in_Twisted_SrTiO3_Membranes (2026)

**Title:** Flexoelectrically Induced Polar Topology in Twisted SrTiO3 Membranes

**Authors:** Isabel Tenreiro, Hugo Aramberri, Víctor Rouco, Gabriel Sánchez-Santolino, Mariona Cabero, Fernando Gallego, David Sánchez-Manzano, José M. González-Calbet, Federico Mompeán, Mar García-Hernández, Alberto Rivera-Calzada, Jorge Íñiguez-González, Carlos León, Jacobo Santamaría

**Journal:** Advanced Materials 2026, e12969

**Abstract:** We report a polar topology in twisted membranes of SrTiO3 (STO), a paraelectric centrosymmetric compound. The polar landscape is generated by the flexoelectrically induced polarization driven by the strain modulation triggered by twisting. This finding opens exciting opportunities for the exploration of novel physical effects and functionalities. The strain and polarization patterns in top and bottom layers are correlated in a way which breaks inversion and mirror symmetries as dictated by the emergent chirality imposed by twisting.

**Keywords:** flexoelectricity, SrTiO3, twisted membranes, polar topology, chirality, ferroelectricity, membranes, twistronics
